Method. A parcel that no longer exists in the current cadastral was reconstructed by taking every current child lot the successor map ties back to it (matched by address) and merging their polygons with a geometric union. The result approximates the original boundary before subdivision. Source: ParcelHistory (Layer 2/3) successor mapping + Cadastral parcel polygons (Layer 4). Davidson County.
