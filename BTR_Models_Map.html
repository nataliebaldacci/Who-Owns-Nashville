Method. Vertically integrated: operator buys land, builds through an in-house GC, and keeps the homes — builder and landlord are one corporate family (AMH via AMH Development Tennessee GC; AHV). Developer-handoff: a builder constructs and transfers finished homes in bulk to a rental operator at certificate of occupancy (Invitation ← Elmington; Tricon ← Meritage; Normandy ← Ole South). Builder / land-banker: supplies the market — builds homes to sell to an operator, or banks entitled land for later construction — rather than being the landlord; pipeline communities sit here until the record shows whether they hold or sell.
