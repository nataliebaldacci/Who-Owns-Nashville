July 1, 2006
Tennessee requires Davidson County landlords to register

The state residential landlord-registration law. Applies only to Davidson County (metropolitan government, population over 500,000). Requires the owner or agent's name, address, and phone, plus each unit's address. Fee capped at $10/year; $50/week penalty for failure to register.

Tenn. Code Ann. § 66-28-107↗ State statute
April 28, 2014
Registration address must be physical, not a PO box

The owner address must be a physical address that "does not include a post office box," and any change of ownership must be reported within 30 days.
