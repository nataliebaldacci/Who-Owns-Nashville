Methodology. Each dot is a Davidson County parcel conveyed by a Trustee's Deed (the instrument that completes a non-judicial foreclosure sale) at some point in its chain of title. The dot turns red in the year of the foreclosure; each later transfer recolors it by the new owner's occupancy — green if owner-occupied (owner mailing zip = property zip), orange if non-owner / investor — and grey when the parcel number ceased to exist (absorbed into a condo Master Deed or re-plat; shown at its successor parcel). The filter selects parcels by their status today. Source: ParcelHistory ownership (Layer 2) + property history (Layer 3) + Cadastral (Layer 4). 2001–2026, Davidson County.
