Method. Every point is a Davidson County home whose recorded chain of title shows it passing between two or more borrower SPVs of the same parent operator (e.g. FREO → Progress Residential 2015-2 Borrower → Progress Residential Borrower 5). 1,606 of these internal moves are Quit Claim Deeds and most are recorded at $0 — paper transfers that repackage homes into successive securitizations rather than sell them. Color = parent; dot size = number of internal transfers. Source: ParcelHistory ownership (Layer 2), full chain of title; parents resolved by the operator name crosswalk. Davidson County.
