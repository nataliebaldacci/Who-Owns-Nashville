Eviction filings keep climbing in Davidson County

Monthly eviction filings, January 2016 to May 2026. Filings fell during the 2020 to 2021 federal moratorium, then rose past their pre-pandemic level. February 2024 set the record at 1,478 filings in a single month. Landlords filed 115,703 evictions across the decade.

Source: Eviction Lab, Eviction Tracking System (Davidson County, TN), https://evictionlab.org/eviction-tracking/ (last accessed July 1, 2026). This dataset counts filings by month for the whole county. It does not name the plaintiff or property owner behind each case, so it cannot be broken out by operator on its own.
