Nashville eviction filings have climbed far above their historical norm

Davidson County eviction filings each year as a percentage above or below the county's historical monthly average. The moratorium years fall well below the line; every year since 2022 rises further above it.

Source: Eviction Lab, Eviction Tracking System (Davidson County, TN), https://evictionlab.org/eviction-tracking/ (last accessed July 1, 2026). Zero marks the historical monthly average. Chart: Natalie Baldacci.
