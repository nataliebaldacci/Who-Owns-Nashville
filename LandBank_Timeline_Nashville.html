Method. Every polygon starts as vacant land (assessor improvement value $0) in 2000. It turns red the year a land-bank entity acquired it, orange the year it was subdivided by plat, and green the year a building first appears (improvement value rises above $0). Across these parcels the order is overwhelmingly acquire → subdivide → build (79% subdivide before any sale), median 2-year hold before the plat. Source: ParcelHistory ownership (Layer 2) + property history (Layer 3) + Cadastral polygons (Layer 4). Davidson County.
