Population-adjusted housing production, Davidson County

New residential units permitted per 1,000 residents per year, by structure type, 1990 to 2025. Modeled after Urban Institute Figure 1. Single-family production fell after the 2008 crash and never returned to its early-2000s pace; multifamily carried most of the post-2015 growth.

Source: U.S. Census Bureau, Building Permits Survey, Davidson County, https://www.census.gov/construction/bps/ (last accessed July 1, 2026); population from U.S. Census Bureau Vintage 2025 Population Estimates, https://www.census.gov/data/tables/time-series/demo/popest/2020s-total-housing-units.html (last accessed July 1, 2026). Chart: Natalie Baldacci. After Karan Kaul, Laurie Goodman & Michael Neal, Urban Institute (Dec. 2021), Figure 1.
