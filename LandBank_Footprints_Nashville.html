Land-Bank Footprints in Davidson County

Each shape is one land banker's full holdings, rebuilt from every parcel it ever owned.

Method. For each land-bank entity, every Davidson parcel its ownership history ever touched was collected and the parcel polygons were merged into one footprint by geometric union. This rebuilds the development tract even after it subdivided into a new street grid with new addresses. Acres computed in Tennessee State Plane. Source: ParcelHistory ownership (Layer 2) + Cadastral parcel polygons (Layer 4). Davidson County.
