How fast Nashville built, and who built it

Single-family new-construction permits in Davidson County by builder type, 2010 to 2025. National production builders surged in the cheap-money window and institutional build-to-rent enters in 2020, peaking in 2021 to 2022, just before higher rates cut the qualified-buyer pool and left new inventory unsold.

Source: Metro Codes Department new-construction permits, classified by builder (86,903 SFR permits, 2010–2025) (last accessed July 1, 2026). Institutional BTR = homes permitted by build-to-rent operators, built to rent rather than sell. Chart: Natalie Baldacci.
