Method. Each shape is a Davidson County parcel whose assessor improvement value was $0 in 2000 — vacant developable land of any class except Open-Space and Exempt (so residential and vacant-multifamily/commercial sites are included). It turns green the year a building first appears (improvement value rises above $0). It is red in the years it was owned by a corporate entity (LLC, LP, Inc, Trust…) and grey when individually owned — ownership taken from the recorded deed history, year by year, not the current owner. Source: Assessment History + ParcelHistory ownership (Layer 2) + Cadastral. Davidson County.
