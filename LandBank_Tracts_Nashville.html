Land banker = an entity (LLC/Inc/LP) that held a vacant residential tract of 5+ acres (assessment improvement value = $0, class Residential) acquired since 2000, kept by its longest-holding owner, and is out-of-state or non-owner-occupied (owner mailing address ≠ the property). Builders, farm families, churches, golf courses, landfills and government are excluded. Acres from ParcelHistory property layer (pre-subdivision tract size). Source: Assessment History + ParcelHistory (L2/L3) + Cadastral (L4). Davidson County.
