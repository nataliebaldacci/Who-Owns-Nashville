Build-to-Rent: Homes That Exited vs. Homes Being Built Now

Where institutional operators are selling out finished rentals versus where they are still building.

Layer
Method. Pipeline = a parcel an institutional operator owns or owned that is currently vacant (assessor improvement value $0), has no land-use yet (NULL land-use = under development), or carries a new-construction permit issued 2023 or later. Exit = a home built while the operator held the land (improvement value rose from $0 at or after acquisition) that is now owned by a non-institutional buyer — the build-to-rent home sold back to the market. Source: Assessment History (improvement value), ParcelHistory ownership chain of title, Single-Family new-construction permits, Cadastral. Davidson County.
