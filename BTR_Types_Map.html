Three Ways to Build-to-Rent in Davidson County

Who builds and keeps, who builds and sells, and who is still banking land.

Build-to-rent type
Operator
Method. Each polygon is a Davidson County parcel tied to a build-to-rent operator or community, classed by business model. Vertically integrated: the operator builds homes on land it owns and keeps them as rentals (AMH, Normandy, Tricon). Merchant-build / forward-flow: a builder constructs and sells finished homes to a rental operator (ECG/CRP, ResiBuilt→Invitation, VineBrook). Land-bank / pre-development: an investor holds entitled land ahead of vertical construction (Kennedy Lewis, TPG, Brookfield). Production builder: a national homebuilder running BTR sections (Pulte, Lennar). Source: per-operator parcel polygons + ParcelHistory chain of title + Assessment. Davidson County.
