Who registers many homes at once: landlord registrations by operator

Each dot is one landlord registration case filed with the Metro Codes Department, placed by the date it was filed and the number of property addresses it covered. Individual landlords sit at the bottom, registering a single home. Institutional operators file registrations covering dozens or hundreds of homes on the same day. American Homes 4 Rent registered 442 addresses on one 2012 case.

Source: Metro Codes Department, Landlord Registration (ePermits caseTypeID 176), https://epermits.nashville.gov/api/permit/1.0/Case (last accessed July 1, 2026); operator attribution via Davidson County deed records. Chart: Natalie Baldacci.
