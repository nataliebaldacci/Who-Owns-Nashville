Davidson County Single-Family Home Sales: New vs. Existing

Single-family home sales in Davidson County, 2012–2024, split into newly built and existing homes. Newly built sales sit at the base of each column; existing sales stack above.

Source: THDA tabulations of Property Assessment Division data, Tennessee Home Sales Data. Figures for 2017 and 2018 reflect THDA methodological revisions; the 2020 new/existing split is a known THDA anomaly (new-home sales undercounted), though the annual total is unaffected.
